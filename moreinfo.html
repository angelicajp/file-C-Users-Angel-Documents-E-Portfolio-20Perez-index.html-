A Short Description About Me!

Hello! I'm Angelica Jade Perez, the oldest in my family, raised by my wonderful parents, Genevieve Perez and Jose Sadjid Perez. I have two siblings: my younger sister Christine and my youngest brother Xian. Being the oldest has taught me a lot about responsibility and caring for others.

I have a variety of interests that keep me engaged and happy. I love reading manhwa, as the intricate stories and beautiful illustrations captivate me. Dancing is another passion of mine, especially hip-hop, which allows me to express myself and stay active. I also enjoy playing video games, as they provide an exciting escape and a way to connect with friends. While I practiced karate for some time, I'm currently on a break from it.

In my downtime, you can often find me on my phone or catching up on sleep—there’s nothing quite like recharging! My love languages are acts of service and physical touch, and I truly value genuine connections with people. I cherish my close-knit group of friends, each of whom means the world to me. Life can be challenging at times, but I believe that having supportive friends and family makes all the difference.
